Egg-Fried-Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of dry long grain white rice
  • ½ of a small yellow onion
  • 1 clove of garlic, 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of pepper
  • 4 tablespoon of low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 ½ tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 Spring onion for garnishing
  • 4 cups of water

Tools:

  • Deep or large pan
  • Small pan for frying egg
  • Spatula
  • Bowls
  • Medium saucepan with lid
  • Measuring cup
  • Chef or paring Knife
  • Measuring spoons
  • Chopping Board

    1. Gather the required ingredients.
    2. Gather the required tools.
    3. Measure 2 cups of rice with 4 cups of water.
    4. Boil the rice.
    5. Dice the onion using the chef or paring knife, while waiting for the rice to be cooked.
    6. Mince the garlic into smaller pieces using chef or paring knife.
    7. Heat 2 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large pan set on a medium flame.
    8. Add onion and garlic to the heated oil along with salt and pepper, stir frying till it turns aromatic.
    9. Scramble eggs with ½ tablespoon of vegetable oil in a separate pan and set aside once done, while waiting for the onions to turn aromatic.
    10. Add the cooked rice in large pan along with onions and garlic.
    11. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, and red pepper flakes along with the rice.
    12. Add the scrambled egg at last after adding all other ingredient.
    13. Constantly stir fry the rice with spatula, for 3 to 4 minutes.
    14. Finally, ENJOY! The hot and tasty fried rice, transferred in a serving plate with spring onion topping.
